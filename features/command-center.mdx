## Supported Channels

The Command Center monitors and responds across four communication channels:

### Voice (Phone Calls)

**AI capabilities:**
- Answers incoming calls 24/7, including after-hours
- Books, reschedules, and confirms appointments
- Answers common questions about services and pricing
- Escalates complex or sensitive issues to staff

**Transcription:**
Every call is transcribed in real-time with speaker diarization (AI vs. Client vs. Staff).

**Example conversation:**
```
[AI] Thank you for calling Etienne Intelligence. How can I help you today?
[Client] Hi, I want to schedule a Botox appointment at your SoHo location.
[AI] I have availability next Tuesday at 10 AM, 2 PM, or 4 PM. Which works best?
[Client] 2 PM works perfectly.
[AI] Wonderful! You are confirmed for Botox at SoHo on Tuesday at 2 PM with Dr. Chen.
```

**Metrics tracked:**
- Call duration
- Sentiment (positive/neutral/negative)
- After-hours flag
- Revenue impact
- Resolution status

### SMS (Text Messaging)

**AI capabilities:**
- Two-way SMS conversations
- Appointment confirmations and reminders
- No-show prevention outreach
- Rebooking campaigns
- Post-visit feedback collection

**Example flow:**
```
[AI] Hi Emma, friendly reminder — your Hydrafacial is in 2 hours 
      at Williamsburg. Still coming?
      
[Client] Yes running a little late though, maybe 10 min

[AI] No problem! We will have everything ready. See you soon!
```

**Response time:** Average 8-12 seconds for AI responses

### Web Chat

**AI capabilities:**
- Embedded chat widget on your website
- Instant answers to pricing and availability questions
- Real-time booking without leaving the site
- Lead capture for follow-up

**Example interaction:**
```
[Client] Hi, is there a first-time client discount?

[AI] Welcome! New clients get 15% off their first treatment. 
     Our most popular intro service is the Hydrafacial at $250 
     (would be $212.50 with your discount). Want to book?
     
[Client] Yes at SoHo please!

[AI] Booked! Wednesday at 11 AM at SoHo Flagship. Welcome to Etienne!
```

**Conversion rate:** 65% of web chat sessions result in bookings

### Social Media

**Platforms supported:**
- Instagram DMs
- Facebook Messenger
- TikTok messages (coming soon)

**AI capabilities:**
- Responds to DMs within seconds
- Handles booking requests
- Answers pre/post-care questions
- Manages negative feedback escalation

**Example:**
```
[Client via Instagram] Saw your before/after pics! How do I book Botox?

[AI] Thanks for the love! I can book you right here. SoHo has 
     openings Thursday at 10 AM and 3 PM. Interested?
     
[Client] 10 AM!

[AI] Booked! Thursday 10 AM at SoHo for Botox ($450). DM me if 
     you need anything else!
```

## Resolution Breakdown

Understand how conversations are resolved:

### AI Resolved (65-80%)
Conversations completed entirely by AI without staff intervention.

**Common scenarios:**
- Standard bookings
- Appointment confirmations
- Rescheduling requests
- Pricing inquiries
- Service information

**Color:** Blue (primary)

### Escalated (15-25%)
Conversations requiring human expertise or judgment.

**Escalation triggers:**
- Medical concerns or adverse reactions
- Billing disputes
- Negative sentiment detection
- VIP client flags
- Complex multi-service requests
- Requests outside normal hours/policies

**Color:** Orange (warning)

**Escalation process:**
1. AI detects escalation need
2. Conversation routed to appropriate team member
3. Full transcript and context provided
4. AI summarizes issue for quick handoff

### Abandoned (5-10%)
Conversations where the client stopped responding.

**Follow-up:**
- AI automatically attempts one callback/message
- After 24 hours, conversation flagged for manual review
- Client added to follow-up campaign

**Color:** Red (destructive)

<Warning>
High abandonment rates (>15%) may indicate AI response quality issues. Review transcripts to identify patterns.
</Warning>

## Key Metrics

### Total Conversations
Count of all customer interactions across all channels in the selected time period.

**What counts as a conversation:**
- Phone call (any duration)
- SMS thread (grouped by client)
- Web chat session
- Social media message thread

**Typical volume:** 200-400 conversations per location per month

### Average Response Time

**Calculation:**
```
Sum of (first response timestamp - inquiry timestamp) / total conversations
```

**Before AI:** 4.2 hours (next business day for after-hours)
**With AI:** 8-45 seconds
**Reduction:** 85-95%

**Impact on conversion:**
- Responses under 1 minute: 78% conversion rate
- Responses 1-60 minutes: 52% conversion rate  
- Responses over 1 hour: 23% conversion rate

### AI Resolution Rate

Percentage of conversations resolved without human intervention.

**Formula:**
```
(AI Resolved / Total Conversations) × 100
```

**Benchmark:** 65-80% depending on business complexity

**Trend to watch:** This should increase over time as AI learns from escalated conversations.

### Revenue at Risk

Total value of appointments and bookings associated with conversations.

**Includes:**
- After-hours inquiries that would have gone to voicemail
- Quick responses that prevented client from calling competitors
- No-show prevention saves
- Rebooking captures

**Example calculation:**
- 47 after-hours bookings × avg $420 = $19,740 revenue at risk
- Without AI: Estimated 75% loss rate = $14,805 saved

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Review escalations within 15 minutes">
    When AI escalates a conversation:
    - Reason is always provided in the summary
    - Full transcript gives complete context
    - Client is told "connecting you now" - they expect quick response
    
    Target: under 15 minutes for standard escalations, under 5 minutes for urgent
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Study AI resolutions to learn">
    Review AI-resolved conversations weekly to:
    - See how AI handles common objections
    - Learn effective booking language
    - Identify knowledge gaps (AI couldn't answer)
    - Improve your own conversion techniques
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Provide feedback on AI performance">
    If AI escalates unnecessarily or makes mistakes:
    - Click "Report Issue" in transcript
    - Describe what should have happened
    - AI model improves from this feedback
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Celebrate after-hours wins">
    Monitor the "After-hours inquiries captured" metric.
    
    This is revenue that was previously lost completely. Share wins with your team to build confidence in the AI system.
  </Accordion>
