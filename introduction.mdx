## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Zenoti">
    Link your Zenoti account using API credentials. The platform syncs your historical data and begins monitoring appointments, guests, and revenue in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI agents activate">
    Nine specialized AI agents start analyzing your data: conversation analysts, no-show predictors, revenue scouts, and more. They work continuously to identify opportunities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get insights">
    View your revenue intelligence dashboard with metrics like total revenue, revenue at risk, no-show rates, and response times—all compared against previous periods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take action">
    Act on AI-generated opportunities: send reminders to high-risk appointments, follow up on missed calls, or optimize your schedule for peak utilization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Data security:** All data transmission is encrypted. Etienne Intelligence adheres to HIPAA guidelines and never shares your business data with third parties.
</Note>

## Key metrics tracked

The platform monitors and analyzes critical business metrics across your organization:

- **Total revenue** — Daily, weekly, and monthly revenue tracking with trend analysis
- **Revenue at risk** — Identified gaps from no-shows, missed calls, and upsell opportunities
- **No-show rate** — Percentage of missed appointments with predictive risk scoring
- **Response time** — Average time to answer customer inquiries across all channels
- **Utilization rate** — Provider and treatment room efficiency percentages
- **AI resolution rate** — Percentage of customer inquiries handled without human intervention
- **New client acquisition** — First-time guests tracked by source and location
- **Rebooking rate** — Return visit percentage by provider and service type
